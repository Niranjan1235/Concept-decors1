About Our Facility

Built to Perfect
Every Detail

Our 45,000 sq.ft manufacturing facility in Bengaluru is the engine behind every Concept Decors project. Every piece of furniture, every cabinet, every custom element is conceived, crafted and quality-checked here — ensuring absolute control over materials, finishes and timelines.

Unlike studios that outsource manufacturing, our integrated factory model means zero middlemen, superior quality and delivery timelines we can guarantee — not just promise.

The Journey

From Vision to a Living Space

Every extraordinary interior follows the same story — a disciplined journey from blank canvas to a space that transforms how you live.

Step 01

Your Vision Begins Here

You arrive with a dream — a space that feels entirely yours. Our design team listens deeply, absorbs your personality, and translates it into detailed mood boards, material palettes, and spatial blueprints.

Step 02

Materials Curated with Intent

From our 2,000+ material library — imported marbles, FSC-certified timbers, brushed brass, Belgian linens — every surface is chosen not just for beauty, but for how it will age, feel, and perform across decades.

Step 03

Precision Engineered In-House

Inside our factory, German CNC routers, dust-free spray booths and 280+ craftsmen bring the design to life with millimetre accuracy — no outsourcing, no middlemen, no compromise on quality.

Step 04

48-Point Quality Gate

Before anything leaves our facility, every unit is subjected to a rigorous 48-point inspection — structural integrity, raking-light finish review, hardware function tests and dimensional checks. Perfection is the only standard.

Step 05

Your Space, Transformed

GPS-tracked vehicles deliver each piece in custom foam-lined crates. Our installation team fits every element on-site with the same obsessive precision — and we don't leave until every detail is exactly right.

How We Make It

The Manufacturing Process

Six precision stages — from raw material to finished masterpiece.

01
01 / 06
Raw Material Sourcing

Premium teak, MDF, plywood, metals and stone sourced from certified suppliers across India and imported from Italy, Germany and Malaysia. Every lot is inspected on arrival.

Next Stage
02
02 / 06
Precision Cutting & Shaping

CNC routers, laser cutters and panel saws ensure millimetre-precise cuts. German-engineered machines handle everything from intricate marquetry to structural joinery panels.

Next Stage
03
03 / 06
Assembly & Joinery

Expert carpenters hand-assemble each piece using traditional mortise-and-tenon techniques and modern concealed hardware for maximum structural durability.

Next Stage
04
04 / 06
Surface Treatment & Finishing

Dust-free spray booths apply PU lacquers, veneers, laminates, duco paints and high-gloss finishes. Colour-matching is guaranteed across every element of a project.

Next Stage
05
05 / 06
Quality Control & Testing

Every unit passes a 48-point check — structural integrity, raking-light finish inspection, hardware function tests and dimensional verification before dispatch approval.

Next Stage
06
06 / 06
Packaging & Delivery

Custom foam-lined crates protect every piece in transit. GPS-tracked vehicles ensure on-time, damage-free arrival — from our factory floor to your project site.

Quality Promise

Zero Compromise
on Quality

Every material, joint and finish is held to standards that exceed industry benchmarks. Our ISO 9001 certified processes and 48-point quality checks mean you receive nothing less than perfection.

Our dedicated QC team of 22 specialists conducts multi-stage inspections — at material intake, mid-production and pre-dispatch — ensuring absolute consistency across every element of your project.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality management certified to international standards across all manufacturing processes.

Eco-Conscious Manufacturing

FSC-certified timber sources and low-VOC finishes — beautiful interiors that respect the planet.

Fire Retardant Certified

German Engineering

Homag CNC machines and Blum hardware systems for precision that never falters across production runs.
